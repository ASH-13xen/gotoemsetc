CODE OF CONDUCT

  1. Professionalism: All employees are expected to maintain a high standard of professionalism in behavior, communication, and appearance. Respect towards colleagues, clients, and stakeholders is mandatory at all times.
  2. Integrity & Honesty :Employees must act with honesty, fairness, and transparency in all business dealings. Misrepresentation, fraud, or misuse of company resources will not be tolerated.
  3. Confidentiality: Employees are required to protect company information, trade secrets, and client data. Sharing confidential information with unauthorized persons is strictly prohibited.
  4. Equal Opportunity & Respect: Discrimination or harassment based on gender, caste, religion, race, age, disability, or any other factor will not be accepted. A culture of inclusion and mutual respect must be upheld.
  5. Use of Company Property: Company assets, including laptops, phones, vehicles, and intellectual property, must be used responsibly and only for official purposes. Any damage, misuse, or loss must be reported immediately.
  6. Conflict of Interest: Employees must avoid situations where personal interests conflict with organizational interests. Any such potential conflict should be disclosed to management.
  7. Workplace Behavior: Harassment, bullying, intimidation, or inappropriate conduct of any kind is strictly prohibited. Employees must contribute to a safe, positive, and supportive workplace.
  8. Compliance with Laws & Policies: All employees are required to comply with applicable laws, labor regulations, and organizational policies at all times.
  9. Substance Abuse & Misconduct: Possession, use, or distribution of illegal substances, alcohol abuse, or reporting to work under the influence is strictly prohibited.
  10. Disciplinary Action: Any violation of this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary measures, which may include verbal/written warnings, suspension, or termination of employment.
