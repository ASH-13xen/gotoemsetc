HR POLICIES

  1. Recruitment & Onboarding Policy
  • All recruitment will follow a fair and transparent process.
  • Background verification and reference checks are mandatory before confirmation.
  • Selected candidates must submit mandatory documents (ID proof, PAN, Aadhaar, Relieving letter).
  • New employees will undergo an induction program to familiarize them with the company’s policies, culture, and processes.
  1. Code of Conduct & Ethics Policy
  • Employees must maintain professionalism in behavior, attire, and communication.
  • Confidential company information must not be disclosed outside the organization.
  • Employees must avoid conflicts of interest (personal business dealings that may affect work).
  • Bribery, corruption, or accepting gifts beyond policy limits is strictly prohibited.
  • Respect and inclusivity must be maintained at all times.
  1. Leave Policy
  • Annual/Privilege Leave: [70] days per year, accrued monthly.
  • Casual Leave: [12] days per year, to cover personal matters. Eligible: After probation
  • Sick Leave: [6] days per year (medical certificate required for >2 days). Eligible: After probation
  • Leave must be applied for and approved in advance, except in emergency.
  1. Attendance & Working Hours Policy
  • Standard working hours are [9:30 AM – 6:30 PM], Monday to Saturday.
  • Employees must adhere to attendance marking via [biometric system/HRMS/Google sheet].
  • Late arrivals beyond [15 minutes] will be recorded and will result in loss of pay.
  • Work-from-home may be allowed with prior manager approval.
  • Overtime will be compensated (per hour) as per company and statutory rules.
  1. Probation & Confirmation Policy
  • All new hires will be on probation for [3-6 months] based on the performance.
  • Performance will be reviewed at the end of probation.
  • Based on performance, employment will be confirmed, extended, or terminated.
  1. Performance Appraisal Policy
  • Performance reviews will be conducted annually (or half-yearly).
  • Appraisals will consider work quality, teamwork, punctuality, leadership, and targets achieved.
  • Salary revisions, promotions, and bonuses will be linked to appraisal outcomes and company performance.
  1. Disciplinary & Grievance Policy
  • Misconduct includes: absenteeism, insubordination, harassment, theft, policy violation.
  • Disciplinary process: verbal warning → written warning → suspension/termination (based on severity).
  • Employees may raise grievances to their manager or HR in writing.
  • All complaints will be investigated fairly and confidentially.
  1. Anti-Harassment & Equal Opportunity Policy
  • The company provides a safe workplace free from discrimination and harassment.
  • Any form of sexual harassment, bullying, or workplace violence will not be tolerated.
  • POSH Internal Complaints Committee will handle harassment complaints confidentially.
  • The company is an equal opportunity employer, prohibiting bias on gender, caste, religion, age, disability, or orientation.
  1. Exit & Relieving Policy
  • Employees must submit a written resignation and serve the notice period of agreed days/months.
  • Early release may be allowed at management’s discretion or with payment in lieu of notice.
  • Full & Final Settlement (salary, leave encashment, statutory dues) will be processed within [Next pay date] of exit.
  • Employees must return all company assets (Laptop, Phone, Documents) before receiving relieving/experience letters.
  1. Client Confidentiality Policy
  • All employees must treat client information as strictly confidential.
  • No client data (financial, personal, or project-related) may be disclosed to unauthorized persons inside or outside the organization.
  • Sharing client information through personal devices, cloud storage, or social media is prohibited.
  • Breach of confidentiality will result in disciplinary action, including possible termination and legal consequences.
  1. Data Protection & ISMS Policy (Information Security Management System)
  • Employees must ensure that company and client data is stored, processed, and transmitted securely.
  • Access to data will be role-based and must not be shared without authorization.
  • Strong passwords, encryption, and secure networks must be used at all times.
  • Personal devices (phones, laptops) should not be used for official data unless approved.
  • Any data breach, hacking attempt, or loss of information must be reported immediately to the IT/HR department.
  1. Interview & Recruitment Policy
  • Recruitment will follow equal opportunity and fair hiring practices.
  • Interviews will be conducted professionally and without bias based on gender, religion, caste, or personal beliefs.
  • Candidate information (CVs, interview notes) must be kept confidential.
  • Final hiring decisions must be approved by [Management/HR authority].
  • Feedback to unsuccessful candidates should be professional and respectful.
  1. Salary & Compensation Policy
  • Employees will be compensated as per the agreed salary structure outlined in their appointment letters.
  • Salary disbursement will take place on [7Th] through bank transfer.
  • Any changes in salary (increments, deductions, bonuses) will be communicated in writing.
  • Salary deductions may apply for absenteeism, late arrivals, or violations of company policy.
  • Performance-based incentives and allowances will be decided by management.
  1. Internal SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) Policy
  • Each department will maintain written SOPs for their operations to ensure consistency and efficiency.
  • SOPs must be followed by all employees without exception.
  • Updates to SOPs will be communicated by department heads and approved by management.
  • Failure to follow SOPs may lead to disciplinary action.
  • Employees may suggest improvements to SOPs, subject to management review.
  • Terms & Conditions of Employment
  1. Employment is subject to company policies, procedures, and legal regulations.
  2. Employees must comply with the Code of Conduct, confidentiality rules, and safety standards.
  3. A minimum notice period of 2-3 months must be served before resignation, unless waived by management.
  4. Company property (laptops, Documents) must be returned upon exit.
  5. Dual employment or working with competitors during employment is strictly prohibited.
  6. The company reserves the right to amend policies as needed and will notify employees accordingly.
  7. Violation of these terms may result in disciplinary action, including termination.
