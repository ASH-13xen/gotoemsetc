1. Ownership of Work Product (Work Made for Hire)

The Creative (Videographer, Video Editor, or Graphic Designer) acknowledges and agrees that all video footage, audio recordings, photographs, graphic designs, illustrations, source files, and related digital assets (collectively, the "Work Product") created or delivered during their engagement with the Organization are considered "works made for hire."

The Organization shall hold the exclusive copyright and all proprietary rights to this Work Product in perpetuity. This includes the right to:

  • Modify: Edit, remix, crop, or repurpose the Work Product.
  • Distribute: Share content across any platform (Social Media, Web, TV, Print, etc.).
  • Archive: Store, backup, or delete data at the Organization's discretion.
  • Waiver: The Creative waives any right to royalties, residuals, or additional compensation for the use of the Work Product.

2. Scope of Assets & Delivery

The Creative agrees to transfer all Work Product and raw assets to the Organization's storage systems upon request. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Video/Audio: Raw footage, b-roll, sound effects, and voiceovers.
  • Design: Vector files (.ai, .eps), Photoshop files (.psd), and final exports (.pdf, .png).
  • Project Files: Premiere Pro (.prproj), After Effects (.aep), or other project-specific software files including all linked assets.

3. Consent, Confidentiality, and Data Management

The Creative provides full consent for the Organization to use the Work Product for internal/external marketing, social media, and commercial distribution. Furthermore, the Creative agrees to:

  • Non-Disclosure: Not leak, sell, or share internal footage, "behind-the-scenes" data, or draft designs without written consent.
  • Data Deletion: Delete all organizational data from personal hard drives or private cloud storage once the project is finalized and successfully transferred to the Organization.
  • Portfolio Use: The Creative may use excerpts of the final Work Product for their professional portfolio only with prior written consent from the Organization.

4. No Further Compensation

The Creative acknowledges that the compensation received for their services is the full and final payment. No further royalties, residuals, or usage fees shall be owed to the Creative for the Organization's use or re-use of the Work Product.
