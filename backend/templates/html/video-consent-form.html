I, {employeeName}, hereby grant GO-TO Friend Pvt. Ltd. (the "Company") and its authorized representatives the absolute right and permission to film, photograph, and record my voice and likeness (the "Media Content") during the course of my employment or engagement with the Company.

By signing this letter, I acknowledge and agree to the following terms:

1. Ownership and Copyright

  • Perpetual Ownership: I agree that the Company is the sole and exclusive owner of all rights, titles, and interests in the Media Content, including the copyright. This ownership is valid for the entire duration of the Company's existence (in perpetuity).
  • Usage Rights: The Company has the right to use, edit, crop, and distribute this Media Content for client projects, marketing, social media, internal training, or any other professional work-related purposes.

2. Status Post-Offboarding

  • Irrevocability: I understand that my consent remains in full force and effect even if my employment with the Company ends for any reason.
  • No Right to Removal: Upon offboarding, I shall not have the right to request the removal, deletion, or "taking down" of any Media Content that was filmed or published during or prior to my departure. The Company retains the right to continue using such materials in their existing or archived form.

3. Safety and Privacy Protections

  • Professional Standard: The Company agrees to use the Media Content in a professional manner. It shall not be used in a way that is defamatory, or that intentionally compromises my personal safety or dignity.
  • Data Protection: The Company will take reasonable steps to ensure that my personal information (outside of my professional likeness) is handled in accordance with privacy laws and is not misused by third parties.

4. Waiver of Compensation

  • I acknowledge that I will not receive any additional financial compensation, royalties, or residuals for the use of this Media Content beyond my standard employment remuneration, unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.

5. Release of Liability

  • I hereby release and discharge the Company and its clients from any and all claims and demands arising out of or in connection with the use of the Media Content, including but not limited to claims for invasion of privacy or right of publicity.
