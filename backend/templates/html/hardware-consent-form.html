2. Terms of Usage

By signing this form, the employee agrees to the following terms:

  • Professional Use Only: Equipment is provided strictly for company-related business. Personal use of high-end equipment is prohibited unless prior written sync is obtained.
  • Care & Maintenance: The employee is responsible for the physical care of the hardware. This includes keeping equipment in provided cases and avoiding exposure to extreme temperatures or liquids.
  • Data Security: Employees must not bypass security settings on phones. Data stored on SSDs/SD cards is company property and should be backed up to the company cloud regularly.
  • Loss or Damage: Any damage, theft, or loss must be reported to the IT/Operations department within 24 hours.

3. Liability & Return Policy

  • Normal Wear & Tear: The company acknowledges that hardware depreciates. Employees are not liable for standard "wear and tear."
  • Negligence: If damage is determined to be the result of gross negligence or intentional misuse, the employee will be held financially responsible for repair or replacement costs.
  • Termination: Upon resignation or termination, all equipment must be returned in person to GO-TO Friend Pvt. Ltd. by the final day of employment. Failure to do so may result in legal action or withholding of final settlements.

4. Third-Party Damage & Custodial Responsibility

  • The assigned employee remains responsible for the device at all times. While the company understands that accidents happen, the employee to whom the hardware is issued remains the primary custodian of the device.
  • Custodial Liability: If a third party, whether a colleague, family member, or stranger, damages the device, the employee who was assigned the hardware. is still held responsible for the equipment's safety.
  • Negligence Assessment: If the damage occurred because the device was left unattended or in an insecure environment, it may be classified as negligence on the part of the employee who was assigned the hardware.
  • Internal Resolution: The company will not mediate financial disputes between employees. If a third party is an employee/ or anybody else, the two parties are encouraged to resolve the replacement or repair costs between themselves, but the company will hold the employee who was assigned the hardware, accountable for the device's return or restoration.
  • Termination: Upon resignation or termination, all equipment listed above must be returned in person by the final day of employment. Failure to return hardware may result in legal action or the withholding of final settlements (subject to local labor laws).

5. SIM Card Legal Terms & Usage

  • Sole Ownership: The SIM and number remain the absolute property of GO-TO Friend Pvt. Ltd. Porting to a personal account is strictly prohibited.
  • SIM-Binding Compliance: Per current telecom mandates, messaging accounts must remain linked to the physical SIM in this device. Removing the SIM for use in a personal device is strictly prohibited.
  • Prohibited Activities: Use of the SIM for spamming, unsolicited commercial calls (UCC), or any illegal digital activity is prohibited under the Telecommunications Act.

6. Anti-Harassment & Nuisance Policy (Strict Compliance)

By accepting this SIM, the employee agrees to the following "Zero Tolerance" policies regarding communication:

  • Anti-Harassment: The employee shall not use the corporate SIM to send any communication (voice, text, or media) that is defamatory, obscene, fruadulent, harassing, or threatening. This includes harassment of colleagues, clients, or members of the public.
  • Nuisance Calls: The employee is prohibited from using the corporate line for "Cold Calling" or "Nuisance Calling" unless specifically authorized as part of their job description. All calls must comply with "Do Not Disturb" (DND) registries.
  • Illegal Recording: The employee must comply with local privacy laws regarding the recording of phone calls. Unauthorized recording for non-business or malicious purposes is a violation of company policy.
  • Indemnification: The employee agrees to indemnify the Company against any legal action, fines, or penalties resulting from the employee’s misuse of the SIM for harassment or nuisance activities.
  • Immediate Reporting: Loss or theft of the SIM must be reported within 2 hours to prevent identity theft or fraudulent "nuisance" usage in the employee's name.
  • Final Settlement: All hardware and SIM cards must be returned in person. Failure to return these items may result in legal action or deductions from final settlements, subject to local labor laws.
